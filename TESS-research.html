TESS magnitude (Tmag): ~8.36 which means its relatively bright in the TESS bandpass (8th magnitude) and easy for TESS to obtain good photometry on.

J-band magnitude: ~7.74 (from 2MASS near-IR) also consistent with a bright star.

Coordinates: approximately RA: 82.827 Dec: 79.009 This places it in the southern sky, in the large LMC-adjacent field TESS observed.
